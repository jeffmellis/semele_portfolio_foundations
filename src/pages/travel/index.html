I have always travelled all around the world. My family always made time to travel and gain life experience in that way. I have been all around North and Central America, Europe, Africa, Asia and Australia (South America is on my list!!). I have been to Austria, Denmark, France, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, England, Northern Ireland, Vatican City, the Bahamas, Belize, Canada (obviously), the Cayman Islands, Cuba, Dominican Republic, Jamaica, Mexico, Puerto Rico, Saint Maarten, the United States, Botswana, South Africa, Indonesia, Japan, Australia and New Zealand! I have also been to a bunch of other countries but never left the airport, so in my book that doesn’t count. I will always save up money to travel, to me it’s an absolute must and a no-brainer to make travel a part of my life. A few of the places that are high up on my list of places that I want to travel to are (but not limited to): Norway, the Maldives, Sri Lanka, Thailand and Turkey.
